# TopRated NZ

TopRated NZ is a premium business directory focused on identifying and showcasing the highest-rated local businesses across New Zealand. We provide curated guides for various industries in major cities like Auckland and Wellington.

## Key Information
- **Website**: https://toprated.nz
- **Purpose**: A discovery platform for top-rated services, dining, and professionals in Aotearoa.
- **Goal**: Connecting New Zealanders with trusted local businesses through verified ratings and reviews.

## Structure
The site is built with a glassmorphism design system using:
- **Typography**: Outfit (headings), Inter (body).
- **Architecture**: Static HTML with data-driven city/category hubs plus handcrafted editorial guide pages.
- **SEO / GEO**: Optimized meta tags, semantic HTML, and crawlable city-first guide structures.
