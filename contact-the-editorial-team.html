How to reach us

For editorial enquiries, email info@toprated.nz. This is the best route for factual correction requests, ranking-methodology questions, asset swaps, and listing accuracy issues.

Email

info@toprated.nz

Best use

Corrections, trust questions, listing-detail updates, and category placement concerns.

Listing form

For new listing submissions, the business form at /contact is usually faster.

What to include in your email

  1. The TopRated page URL.
  2. The business name involved.
  3. A short explanation of the issue or request.
  4. The corrected information or desired asset.
  5. Evidence where relevant, especially for factual business-detail changes.

What this contact route is for

  • Wrong logo, image, website, or contact detail.
  • Outdated listing descriptions or page facts.
  • Questions about TopRated's editorial policy or ranking methodology.
  • Concerns about category fit or misclassification.
For new business submissions If you want to be considered for a new listing, use the TopRated business submission form as the primary route, then use editorial email if a factual follow-up is needed.
