Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best area in Auckland for Japanese food?

The Auckland CBD, particularly the Britomart and Federal Street precincts, hosts several high-end and contemporary Japanese restaurants. Ponsonby is also a top destination for refined, chef-led Japanese dining.

Are there authentic Izakayas in Auckland?

Yes, Auckland has several authentic izakayas. Tanuki's Cave on Queen Street is a local institution, and Azabu in Ponsonby offers a unique Nikkei (Japanese-Peruvian) twist on the izakaya experience.

Where can I find the best Japanese fine dining in Auckland?

For a premium experience, MASU by Nic Watt at SkyCity and Cocoro in Ponsonby are widely considered the gold standard for Japanese fine dining and degustation in the city.
