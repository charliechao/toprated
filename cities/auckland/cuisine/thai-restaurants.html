Thai Restaurants
in Auckland

Experience the perfect balance of sweet, sour, salty, and spicy at Auckland's premier Thai restaurants. From the aromatic green curries of family-run neighborhood gems to the bold, modern flavors of trendy CBD eateries, Auckland offers an incredible diversity of Thai cuisine that stays true to its fragrant roots while embracing fresh New Zealand produce.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most popular Thai restaurants in Auckland?

Saan in Ponsonby is highly rated for its Northern and Northeastern Thai street food. Kiin Thai in Mt Eden and Blue Elephant in Parnell are also local favorites for traditional flavors.

Where can I find 'actually spicy' Thai food in Auckland?

Many authentic spots like Zap 2 in Dominion Road or the food stalls in the Elliot Stables allow you to request 'Thai Spicy' levels for a truly fiery experience.

Does Auckland have Thai fusion or modern Thai dining?

Yes, Saan offers a refined, modern take on Thai cuisine, while many contemporary Asian fusion restaurants in the CBD incorporate strong Thai influences into their menus.
