Frequently Asked Questions

Which area in Auckland is best for authentic Indian food?

Sandringham Road is widely considered Auckland's 'Little India,' home to numerous authentic eateries like Satya and Paradise Indian Food. The CBD also hosts premium options like Cassia for modern Indian cuisine.

Are there good vegetarian Indian options in Auckland?

Absolutely. Most Indian restaurants in Auckland offer extensive vegetarian menus. Satya and Saravanaa Bhavan are particularly renowned for their authentic South Indian vegetarian specialties.

What is the best modern Indian restaurant in Auckland?

Cassia, located in SkyCity, is the gold standard for modern Indian dining in Auckland, blending traditional flavors with contemporary techniques and local New Zealand ingredients.
