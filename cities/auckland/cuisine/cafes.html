Cafes
in Auckland

Auckland's café culture is thriving, with specialty coffee roasters and artisanal eateries dotting every neighborhood. From waterfront brunch spots in the Viaduct to hidden gems in Kingsland and Ponsonby, the city's cafés offer everything from expertly crafted flat whites to innovative all-day menus. Whether you're after a quick espresso or a leisurely weekend brunch, Auckland's top-rated cafés deliver exceptional quality and atmosphere.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best cafés in Auckland CBD?

The CBD offers excellent options including Orphans Kitchen, Federal Delicatessen, and Little & Friday. Britomart also has a concentration of high-quality specialty coffee spots.

Where can I find the best brunch in Auckland?

Ponsonby Road and the Viaduct are renowned for brunch. Popular spots include Dizengof, Ortolana, and The Store, all offering innovative menus and quality coffee.

Which Auckland cafés roast their own coffee?

Several top cafés roast their own beans, including Allpress Espresso, Flight Coffee, and Kokako. These roasters also supply many other Auckland cafés with specialty beans.
