Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I find the best handmade pasta in Auckland?

Amano in Britomart and Prego on Ponsonby Road are famous for their exceptional handmade pasta. Baduzzi in Wynyard Quarter also offers exquisite pasta and Italian-inspired seafood.

Which Auckland Italian restaurants are best for dates?

Prego is a long-standing favorite for its romantic atmosphere and consistent quality. Non Solo Pizza in Parnell also offers a beautiful courtyard setting perfect for intimate dinners.

Are there authentic wood-fired pizzerias in Auckland?

Yes, Dante's Pizzeria (with multiple locations) is certified by the Associazione Verace Pizza Napoletana. That's Amore in the CBD also provides a very authentic Neapolitan pizza experience.
