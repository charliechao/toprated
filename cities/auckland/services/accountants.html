Frequently Asked Questions

How much does an accountant cost in Auckland?

Auckland accountant fees range from $150–$300 per hour for individual tax returns, $1,500–$5,000+ for annual business accounts, and $200–$400 per hour for specialist advisory work. Many firms offer fixed-fee packages for standard services.

What should I look for in an Auckland accountant?

Look for Chartered Accountant (CA) designation from CA ANZ, experience in your industry, proactive tax planning (not just compliance), cloud accounting expertise (Xero, MYOB), and strong client reviews. A good accountant should save you more than they cost.

When is tax return season in New Zealand?

The New Zealand tax year runs 1 April to 31 March. Individual tax returns are due by 7 July, though extensions are available through tax agents. Top Auckland accountants file returns year-round and help with provisional tax planning.
