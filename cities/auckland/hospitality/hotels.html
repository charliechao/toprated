Auckland offers accommodation for every style and budget, from luxury waterfront hotels to boutique properties in the inner suburbs. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best area to stay in Auckland?

The Auckland CBD and Viaduct are ideal for first-time visitors, offering easy access to attractions, restaurants, and harbor views. Ponsonby and Parnell are great for a more residential, boutique experience.

Which Auckland hotels have the best harbour views?

The Sofitel Auckland Viaduct Harbour, Hilton Auckland, and Park Hyatt Auckland all offer spectacular harbor and city views. The Park Hyatt, in particular, is renowned for its panoramic vistas.

Are there luxury boutique hotels in Auckland?

Yes, Auckland has several excellent boutique options including Hotel DeBrett, The Hotel Britomart, and The Convent Hotel in Ponsonby, each offering unique character and personalized service.
