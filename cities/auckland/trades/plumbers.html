Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a plumber cost in Auckland?

Auckland plumbers typically charge between $100-$150 per hour for standard work, with emergency callouts costing more. Many offer fixed-price quotes for specific jobs like tap replacements or hot water cylinder installations.

Do Auckland plumbers offer 24/7 emergency service?

Yes, many top-rated Auckland plumbers provide 24/7 emergency services for urgent issues like burst pipes, blocked drains, and hot water failures. It's worth keeping a reliable emergency plumber's number on hand.

What should I look for in an Auckland plumber?

Choose a registered Master Plumber with good reviews, transparent pricing, and proper insurance. Check they're licensed for the specific work (plumbing, gasfitting, drainlaying) and offer guarantees on their workmanship.
