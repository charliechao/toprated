Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a registered electrician in Auckland?

Yes, all electrical work in New Zealand must be completed by a registered electrician. DIY electrical work is illegal and dangerous. Always verify your electrician's registration with the Electrical Workers Registration Board.

How much do electricians charge in Auckland?

Auckland electricians typically charge $90-$130 per hour, with call-out fees of $80-$150. Many offer fixed-price quotes for standard jobs like installing light fixtures, power points, or switchboard upgrades.

Can Auckland electricians install EV chargers?

Yes, many Auckland electricians are qualified to install home EV charging stations. This typically requires a dedicated circuit and may need a power upgrade depending on your existing electrical capacity.
