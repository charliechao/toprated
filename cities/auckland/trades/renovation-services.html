Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a kitchen renovation cost in Auckland?

Auckland kitchen renovations range from $25,000 for a basic update to $80,000+ for high-end complete makeovers. Costs depend on cabinetry quality, appliances, benchtops, and structural changes required.

Do I need consent for renovations in Auckland?

Many renovations require building consent from Auckland Council, especially if modifying structure, plumbing, or electrical. Minor cosmetic work often doesn't need consent, but always verify specific requirements with your renovation specialist or the council.

How long does a typical renovation take in Auckland?

Bathroom renovations typically take 3-6 weeks, kitchens 4-8 weeks, and full house renovations 3-6 months. Factor in 6-12 weeks for council consents before work begins on projects requiring approval.
