Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose a good builder in Auckland?

Look for Master Builders members with proven track records, recent references, and comprehensive insurance. Verify they're licensed for your project type and ask to see examples of similar completed work.

What is the average cost of building in Auckland?

New builds in Auckland typically cost $2,500-$4,000+ per square meter depending on specifications and location. Renovations vary widely based on scope, but budget at least $1,500-$2,500 per square meter for quality work.

How long does it take to build a house in Auckland?

A standard new build in Auckland takes 6-12 months from foundation to completion, plus 2-4 months for council consents. Complex designs, weather delays, or material shortages can extend timelines.
