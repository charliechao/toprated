Frequently Asked Questions

How much does house painting cost in Auckland?

Exterior house painting in Auckland typically costs $15,000-$35,000 depending on size and condition. Interior painting ranges from $25-$45 per square meter. Get multiple quotes and ensure they include all prep work and materials.

What's the best time to paint a house in Auckland?

Late spring through early autumn (October-April) offers the most consistent weather for exterior painting in Auckland. Interior work can be done year-round, though summer provides better ventilation and faster drying times.

Do painters include prep work in their quotes?

Reputable Auckland painters include surface preparation (sanding, filling, priming) in their quotes. Be wary of quotes that don't specify prep work, as this is crucial for quality results and longevity.
