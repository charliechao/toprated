Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a car service cost in Wellington?

A basic car service in Wellington costs $150–$250, intermediate service $250–$400, and full service $400–$600+. European vehicles typically cost more. Always ask for a detailed quote before authorising work.

Where can I get a WoF in Wellington?

WoF inspections are available from VTNZ stations and authorised inspection organisations across Wellington including the CBD, Petone, Lower Hutt, Upper Hutt, and Porirua. Most top-rated mechanics also offer WoF inspections — expect to pay $50–$70.

Do Wellington mechanics offer courtesy cars?

Many top-rated Wellington mechanics offer courtesy cars or can arrange drop-off/pick-up services, particularly for longer repair jobs. Ask when booking your appointment.
