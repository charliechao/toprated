Frequently Asked Questions

Where are the main car dealerships in Wellington?

Wellington's main dealer strips are along Hutt Road (Petone to Lower Hutt) for new and used franchises, Courtenay Place area for boutique dealers, and the Porirua/Kapiti corridor for larger independent yards with extensive stock.

Can I negotiate car prices in Wellington?

Yes, there is usually room to negotiate, especially on used vehicles. Research market values on Trade Me and compare prices across dealers. End of month and end of financial year (March) often yield the best deals.

What warranty should I expect from a Wellington car dealer?

All dealers must comply with the Consumer Guarantees Act. Additionally, reputable Wellington dealers offer mechanical warranties from 3 months to 3 years. New car franchises include manufacturer warranties of 3–7 years.
