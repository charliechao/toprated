Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best hotel areas in Wellington?

The waterfront and CBD offer the most popular hotel locations — walking distance to everything. Thorndon is quieter and close to Parliament, while Te Aro (Cuba Quarter) puts you in the heart of the café and bar scene.

How much do hotels cost in Wellington?

Wellington hotel rates range from $120–$200/night for 3-star properties, $200–$350 for 4-star, and $350–$600+ for premium boutique and 5-star hotels. Rates spike during major events like WOW, the NZ Festival, and Beervana.

When is the best time to visit Wellington?

Summer (December–February) offers the best weather, but Wellington's arts festivals in February–March and October–November draw big crowds. Winter rates are lower but expect wind and rain. Book well ahead for any major event period.
