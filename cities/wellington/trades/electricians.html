Frequently Asked Questions

What is the hourly rate for an electrician in Wellington?

Registered electricians in Wellington generally charge between $90 and $125 per hour, excluding GST. Most residential jobs also include a call-out fee or a minimum first-hour charge.

Can I do my own electrical work in Wellington?

No, most electrical work in New Zealand must be performed by a registered electrician for safety and insurance purposes. DIY electrical work can void your house insurance and poses significant fire risks.

Do Wellington electricians install EV chargers?

Yes, many certified electricians in Wellington specialize in smart EV charger installations for residential garages and commercial parking spaces, ensuring your home's power capacity is sufficient.
