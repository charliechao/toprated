Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a home renovation cost in Wellington?

Wellington renovation costs range from $2,000–$4,000 per square metre depending on scope and site complexity. Kitchen renovations start from $25,000–$60,000, bathroom renovations from $20,000–$40,000, and full house renovations from $150,000–$500,000+. Hillside properties and earthquake strengthening add to costs.

Do I need consent for renovations in Wellington?

Most significant renovations require building consent from Wellington City Council, especially structural changes, plumbing, drainage, and electrical work. Heritage-listed properties may also need resource consent. Your renovation company should manage the entire consent process.

How long do renovations take in Wellington?

Bathroom renovations take 3–6 weeks, kitchens 4–8 weeks, and full home renovations 3–8 months. Wellington's weather can impact exterior work timelines. Allow 4–8 weeks for council consent processing before work begins.
