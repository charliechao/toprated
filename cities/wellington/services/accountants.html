Frequently Asked Questions

How much does an accountant cost in Wellington?

Wellington accountant fees range from $150–$280 per hour for individual tax returns, $1,200–$4,500+ for annual business accounts, and $180–$380 per hour for advisory work. Many firms offer fixed-fee packages for standard compliance services.

What should I look for in a Wellington accountant?

Look for Chartered Accountant (CA) status, experience with your industry (especially if government-related), Xero or MYOB certification, proactive communication, and strong Google reviews. A good Wellington accountant should understand the capital's unique business environment.

Do I need a local Wellington accountant?

While accountants can work remotely, a local Wellington practice offers face-to-face meetings, understanding of regional business conditions, and local network connections that can benefit your business. This is especially valuable for Wellington's government contracting sector.
