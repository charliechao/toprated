Frequently Asked Questions

What is the current cost to build a house in Christchurch?

New build costs in Christchurch generally start from $2,800 per square meter for standard specifications and can exceed $4,500 per square meter for high-end architectural homes.

How long does a building project take in Christchurch?

A standard three-bedroom home typically takes 8 to 12 months to build from the foundation stage, plus several months for planning and council consents through Christchurch City Council.

Should I hire a Master Builder in Christchurch?

Yes, hiring a Master Builder provides access to the 10-year Master Build Guarantee, which is the most common and robust protection for homeowners in New Zealand.
